Gamepad


At the moment (v0.2) only some basic "hard-wired" gamepad support in include in ApplePi.   A dialog to configure gamepads will be included in the next release.

The Apple II supported four button and four analog ("paddle") inputs.
Current gamepad-to-Apple II mapping:

Apple II             Gamepad

Paddle 0           Left stick X-axis
Paddle 1           Left stick Y-axis
Paddle 2           Right stick X-axis
Paddle 3           Right stick Y-axis
Switch 0           Left trigger
Switch 1           Left bumper
Switch 2           Right trigger
Switch 3           Right bumper
